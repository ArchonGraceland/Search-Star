How to think about your price

Public — price low. This is discovery. You want platforms pulling your skills and headline data freely so you show up everywhere. Think of it as advertising cost you get paid for instead of paying.
Private — price for value. Platforms accessing your full profile (financials, Presence, media) are getting verified data they'd otherwise buy from brokers at 2–5x your price. You're the cheaper and better source.
Marketing — price for your time. This is attention economics. A $5 message filters out spam. A $50 message means they're serious. A message costs what it costs — no refunds. The identity requirement protects you, and the price protects your attention.

How It Works

1
Public Data Flows
Any platform can query your public tier. Skills, name, headline Presence score. They pay per query, no approval needed.
2
Private Access Request
Platforms wanting the full picture submit a request. You see who, why, and what data they want. Approve or deny.
3
Marketing Inbox
Anyone can message you by paying your price. Verified sender, 500 chars, no refunds. Your attention, your market.
4
Instant Settlement
All revenue — queries and messages — settles to your account in real time. Full audit log. Revoke any key, block any sender.
