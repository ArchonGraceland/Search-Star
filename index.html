Create Your Profile

Your profile is assigned a permanent number (SS-XXXXXX), hosted at a URI you control, and queryable by any platform you approve. Here's how it works.
1
Register
Choose your handle and verify your identity via KYC. You receive a permanent profile number (SS-XXXXXX) and a DID.
2
Build
Populate your profile: financial percentiles (verified via Plaid/ZK proofs), Presence Composite, skills, and interests across athletic, social, and intellectual domains.
3
Price
Set your per-query price for each tier: Public (skills + interests), Private (full profile), and Marketing (pay to message you). Comparable pricing from similar profiles helps you calibrate.
4
Earn
Platforms query your API. Dating apps, recruiters, brands, investors — each query is metered, each message is paid. Revenue settles to your account in real time.
