The Problem
Your data is worth thousands. You see none of it.

Every dating app, recruiter, and social platform harvests your personal data — your income signals, your interests, your photos, your location — and sells access to advertisers without paying you a cent.

The average person's data generates $200–$600 per year in advertising revenue. High-value profiles — high net worth, specific interests, verified credentials — generate far more. All of it goes to the platform.

Search Star flips this. You host your own data. You set the price. Platforms pay you directly.

How It Works
Three steps. You keep everything.
No subscriptions, no middlemen, no data brokers. You create a profile, set your price, and earn every time someone queries it.
1

Create

Build your profile using any AI. Financial standing as age-cohort percentiles (never raw dollars), a Presence Composite score, your skills and credentials, and your interests across athletic, social, and intellectual domains. You approve every field.

2

Price

Set your per-query price for three tiers: Public (skills + interests), Private (full profile including financials and Presence), and Marketing (anyone can message you, but they pay for the privilege). Your attention has a price.

3

Earn

Platforms query your API. Dating apps pull your Presence Composite. Recruiters check your skills. Brands target your advertising profile. Every query is metered, every message is paid. Revenue settles to your account in real time.

What's In a Profile
Four dimensions. Zero raw data exposed.
Every field is designed to be valuable to queriers without making you vulnerable. Percentiles instead of dollars. Brand-blind scoring. You approve every advertising signal.
📊

Financial Standing

Net worth, income, savings rate, credit score, and debt-to-income — all expressed as percentiles within your 5-year age cohort. A 28-year-old at the 90th percentile and a 55-year-old at the 90th percentile are directly comparable. No dollar amounts ever leave your profile.
Verified via zero-knowledge proofs (ZK-SNARKs) through Plaid, IRS, and credit bureau integrations.
✨

Presence Composite

Three dimensions — Rizz (interpersonal magnetism), Vibe (aesthetic taste and curation), and Drip (visual style). The scoring is deliberately brand-blind: visible logos are penalized, originality is boosted, and a $40 vintage find that fits perfectly outscores a $4,000 logo tee.
Scored from photos, peer reviews, space aesthetics, taste signals, and conversation samples.
🔧

Skills & Interests

Professional skills with W3C Verifiable Credentials. Interests across three domains — athletic, social, and intellectual — that serve as discovery signals for platforms and people looking for relevant profiles.
Credentials verified via GitHub, LinkedIn, certifications. Interests populated from Strava, Goodreads, Spotify.
🎯

Advertising Profile

High-value targeting signals you explicitly approve: life stage signals, consumption categories, professional openness, and demographic value tags. Designed to make your attention valuable to advertisers without exposing you to discrimination or exploitation.
Hard guardrails: no medical data, no specific financial targets, no relationship details. You control every signal.
Trust Layer
Your profile is backed by real money.

Anyone can claim to be in the 95th percentile. Search Star profiles are different — trusted people stake their own money that your claims are accurate.

You invite validators — friends, colleagues, collaborators — who review specific sections of your profile and put money behind their vouching. If your data is proven wrong, their stakes are forfeited to refund advertisers.

The more money staked against your profile, the higher your trust score, and the more you can charge per query. It's a prediction market on your own truthfulness.

👥

You invite validators

Only people you approve can validate. A colleague validates your skills. A friend validates your interests. A family member validates life stage.

💰

They stake money

Each validator chooses how much to stake — $1 to $10,000. Higher stakes = higher trust signal. Funds held in escrow.

🤖

Automated checks verify

Credit bureaus, IRS transcripts, GitHub, Plaid — automated sources confirm your claims without revealing raw data.

⚖️

Disputes have consequences

If a claim is proven false, validators forfeit their stakes: 70% refunds advertisers, 20% platform fee, 10% to the reporter.
