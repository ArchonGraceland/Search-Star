## Format legend

Every segment has two aligned elements: a **slide window** and a **voice window**.

```
[SLIDE n — mm:ss–mm:ss]  Title or on-screen text. Slide is visible
                          for this entire window.

VOICE (mm:ss.s–mm:ss.s): "The spoken line."
```

**Hard rules:**
- Voice windows sit **inside** slide windows, never spanning a cut.
- Every slide transition has a **0.5–1.0s silent gap** — the cut lands in silence, then the new line speaks.
- The final slide has a **HOLD**: stay on the frame for 3s after the last line ends, then fade to CTA card.

This format makes voice/slide desync impossible to miss in production. If a take doesn't have a beat of silence at a cut, re-time before shipping.

## Script

[SLIDE 1 — 00:00–00:18]  *Title card:* **How Search Star Works**  *Subtitle:* A platform for sustained practice, witnessed by people with real stakes.

VOICE (00:02.0–00:16.5): "Most platforms reward posting about a practice more than the practice itself. Search Star is built the opposite way. The people who vouch for you have real stakes — and that changes everything downstream."

[SLIDE 2 — 00:18–01:08]  *Header:* **The 90-Day Commitment.**  *Bullets:* Practice you choose. 14-day launch window. Day 15 is the start ritual. Day 90 is release.

VOICE (00:19.5–01:06.5): "A commitment on Search Star is ninety days of real practice. You choose the practice — woodworking, running, a language, serious reading, anything that takes sustained effort. Before day one there's a fourteen-day launch window. That's the time you use to share your commitment with the people who believe in it. On day fifteen, you perform a start ritual — a written statement of what you're beginning. The clock begins the moment you submit it. Ninety days of work follows."

[SLIDE 3 — 01:08–02:18]  *Header:* **Sponsors are the witnesses.**  *Three lines:* They pledge money when they join. One button releases it at day 90. One button vetoes the streak at any time.

VOICE (01:10.0–02:16.5): "Sponsors are anyone who believes in your commitment enough to put money behind it. Family, friends, colleagues, an institution that works alongside your personal sponsors. When a sponsor joins, they pledge an amount — any amount, from five dollars to five thousand — and it goes on hold. They get a private read-only link to your commitment. They watch the ninety days unfold. At day ninety, they release the payment. Or, at any point in the window, any one of them can veto the streak with a single action. If that happens, the streak ends. You bail and restart from a new launch period. The rule is deliberate: losing one sponsor mid-streak means losing the streak. Maintaining those relationships across ninety days is itself part of the practice."

[SLIDE 4 — 02:18–03:08]  *Header:* **The Companion.**  *Three short lines, stacked:* No grade. No authority. Reflects what it sees.

VOICE (02:19.5–03:06.5): "Alongside the two human roles there's an AI Companion. It accompanies you through the ninety days — asks what a good teacher would ask, notices patterns across sessions, helps with documentation when you want help. The Companion holds no authority. It cannot confirm practice, advance your Trust Stage, or determine completion. At day ninety, it writes a summary for your sponsors — a reading aid they use to decide whether to release payment. The Companion reflects. The sponsors attest. The line is clean."

[SLIDE 5 — 03:08–03:40]  *Header:* **Your Trust Record.**  *Graphic:* five stages, Seedling → Rooting → Growing → Established → Mature.

VOICE (03:09.5–03:38.5): "Trust on Search Star is a growth stage, not a score. Everyone begins at Seedling. Completed sponsored streaks — streaks where every sponsor who pledged released payment at day ninety — are what grow the record. There are no shortcuts, no purchased stages, no leaderboards. The only path forward is to do the work and earn the continued belief of people who know you."

[SLIDE 6 — 03:40–03:55]  *Closing card:* **What do you want to practice?**  *Footer:* searchstar.com

VOICE (03:42.0–03:52.0): "What do you want to practice? Name it. Invite your sponsors. Begin."

[HOLD 3s, fade to CTA card]
